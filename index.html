Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning


AI (Artificial Intelligence) : Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, encompassing tasks like problem-solving, learning, and decision-making.

ML (Machine Learning) : Machine Learning is a subset of AI focused on enabling computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

Semblance of AI & ML in Cyber Security


Enhanced Threat Detection: AI and ML technologies bolster cybersecurity efforts by analyzing large datasets to pinpoint patterns and anomalies that could signal potential cyber threats. This proactive approach enables early detection and prevention of security breaches before they escalate.

Automated Incident Response: AI-powered systems automate incident response processes by swiftly identifying and mitigating security breaches. This rapid response minimizes the impact of cyber attacks and reduces the time required to mitigate threats, thereby enhancing overall cybersecurity resilience.
